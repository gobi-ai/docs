Gobi is built on **Personal Context Management (PCM)** — the evolution of how we relate to knowledge. Where earlier tools focused on storing and organizing notes, PCM shifts the work to the agent. Your Brain doesn't wait to be organized. It learns, connects, and surfaces what matters — continuously, in the background.
