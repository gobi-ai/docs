## The Gobi Paradigm

Most tools ask you to organize your knowledge. Gobi's agent does it for you. This shift — from Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) to Personal Context Management (PCM) — is the foundation of everything Gobi builds.

The Brain is the core primitive. Every Gobi product either feeds the Brain, reads from it, or shares it.
