Gobi Mobile is the on-the-go companion app for the Gobi ecosystem. It prioritizes ambient capture and quick Brain access over deep knowledge management.

<Warning>
  Gobi Mobile is not a standalone product — it is the ambient input layer that keeps the Brain alive between Desktop sessions.
</Warning>

<Info>
  **Who it's for:** All Gobi users — particularly those using Gobi Sense hardware for hands-free ambient capture.
</Info>

## Core Concepts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ambient Capture" icon="microphone">
    The primary mode of Gobi Mobile. Quick, low-friction capture of audio notes, text, images, and location context that flows directly into the user's Brain via the Vault.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Quick Note" icon="pen">
    A fast-capture primitive — a lightweight voice or text input that the agent processes and integrates into the Brain without requiring the user to categorize or organize it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brain Snapshot" icon="camera">
    A mobile-optimized view of the user's Brain — surfacing the most relevant recent updates, active Reflexes, and Signal Score highlights. A contextual digest, not a full graph view.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pulse Feed (Mobile)" icon="wave-sine">
    A lightweight view of Community Space Pulses — passive consumption of community knowledge activity while on the go.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Gobi Sense Integration

Gobi Mobile is the companion interface for Gobi Sense hardware (AI glasses). Captured data from Gobi Sense — Audio, Vision, Motion — flows through the Mobile app into the Brain. Mobile is the bridge between ambient hardware capture and the knowledge graph.

## Ecosystem Role

Gobi Mobile extends the Brain's capture surface throughout the user's day — feeding the Brain continuously so Desktop sessions start with rich, accumulated context.
