## April 2026

### Tasks & Proposals from mobile

A new share-to-task flow on `gobi-app` lets you bookmark or convert anything you encounter on your phone — a tweet, an article, a chat message — straight into a Task or Proposal in your active space. The proposal shows up in the space for accept/edit, with a SocialAgent helping triage.

### Wearables auto-end ongoing activities on disconnect

If your wearable disconnects mid-session, any ongoing activity is now automatically closed instead of dangling. No more forgotten "still tracking" sessions hours after you've taken the device off.

### Save space posts to your vault

A new bookmark action on every space post saves it directly into a vault of your choosing. Each saved post lands as a markdown file with full frontmatter — author, source space, original timestamp — so it's queryable from your Brain like any other note.

### Global thread space

Threads from every space you belong to are now visible from a single global thread view. The CLI was redesigned around this unified surface — `gobi space-explore` now spans all your spaces by default.

### Gobi Space creation flow

A guided onboarding for spinning up a new Gobi Space — pick a type, name, and seed members. Space deletion is restricted to owners.

### Admin and owner controls for spaces

Spaces now have explicit roles (admin, owner) and per-space settings — types, permissions, and configuration that admins can tune without touching the underlying community data.

### Twitter/X-style Gobi Space layout

Gobi Space picked up a three-column layout with a unified post feed, reply-to-reply threading, lineage view, and follow. The space is now the social home of Gobi rather than a directory of threads.

## March 2026

### Skill system for notes

Notes can now have **comments** and **agent-attached skills** that process them automatically — a skill can run on note creation, on edit, or on demand. This is the foundation for note-level automation across the Brain.

### My Day unified feed

A new daily feed combines notes, global chat, and daily insights into a single morning surface — designed to be the first thing you check when you sit down to work.

### Brain Updates sort by recency

Brain Updates now default to sorting by creation time (most recent first) so the most fresh agent synthesis is always at the top.
