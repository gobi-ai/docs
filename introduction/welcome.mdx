Most AI tools answer your questions. Gobi does something different — it accumulates your knowledge, continuously, in the background. Your Brain grows whether you're actively using it or not.

## What is Gobi?

Gobi is an Ambient AI ecosystem built around a simple idea: the best knowledge management system is one you don't have to manage.

Traditional tools like Obsidian, Notion, or Roam put the organizational burden on you. You tag, link, file, and maintain. The moment you stop, the system degrades.

Gobi shifts that burden to an AI agent. Your **Brain** — Gobi's core data structure — is maintained by the agent, not by you. It captures what you experience, connects what you know, and surfaces what matters when it matters.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Capture">
    Everything flows into your Brain — voice notes, ambient audio, documents, highlights, and manual inputs. Capture happens on Desktop, Mobile, or through Gobi Sense (AI glasses).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    Your agent processes every capture automatically. It summarizes, connects, tags, and ranks — producing Brain Updates that keep you informed without requiring manual review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Surface">
    The agent surfaces what's relevant to your current context via Signal Score — a relevance metric that determines what matters right now, not just what was saved last.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share">
    Your Brain can feed into Community Space — a shared intelligence layer where communities build collective knowledge together.
  </Step>
</Steps>
