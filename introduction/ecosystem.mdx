Every Gobi product either feeds the Brain, reads from it, or shares it. No product operates independently.

## The Brain is the center

The **Brain** is Gobi's core data structure — a living, agent-maintained knowledge graph that grows continuously as you capture, think, and work. Every product in the Gobi ecosystem connects to it.

## How they connect

| Product | Role | Feeds | Reads from |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gobi Desktop | Brain-building workspace | Brain / Vault | Brain / Vault |
| Gobi Personal Space | Public knowledge sharing | — | Brain (selected) |
| Gobi Community Space | Collective intelligence | Community Brain | Member Brains |
| Gobi CLI | Programmatic brain access | Brain / Vault | Brain |
| Gobi Mobile | Ambient capture layer | Brain / Vault | Brain (digest) |

## Key principles

**Agents do the work.** Users interact at the surface. The agent handles organization, connection, and surfacing in the background — continuously, without requiring manual maintenance.

**Privacy by default.** Your Vault never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it. What flows into Personal Space or Community Space is always user-controlled.

**Community is the network effect.** Community Space is not a product people pay for in isolation — it is the reason to stay. The acquisition unit is a community, not an individual.
