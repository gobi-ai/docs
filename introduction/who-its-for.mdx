Gobi is currently in early access, working with three types of users.

## PKM & PCM Power Users

You've tried Obsidian, Notion, Roam, or Logseq. You've built systems, maintained vaults, and spent more time organizing your knowledge than actually using it. You know the value of a second brain — you're just tired of being the one who has to maintain it.

Gobi is built for you. The agent takes over the organizational work so your Brain grows whether you tend to it or not.

**What Gobi does for you:**
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ambient capture" icon="microphone">
    Capture thoughts, meetings, and moments without stopping what you're doing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic organization" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    The agent tags, connects, and summarizes — no manual filing required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Relevant surfacing" icon="signal">
    Signal Score surfaces what matters now, not just what was saved last.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Always-on Brain" icon="brain">
    Your knowledge graph grows continuously, even when you're not actively using it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Community Managers

You run a knowledge community — a Discord, a KakaoTalk group, a cohort, a membership. Your members share valuable knowledge every day, but it disappears into the chat history. You have no way to make it searchable, queryable, or useful over time.

Gobi Community Space turns your community's conversation history into a living knowledge base — indexed, searchable, and agent-accessible.

**What Gobi does for you:**
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Community archive" icon="database">
    Import KakaoTalk and community data as structured, searchable thread posts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Space Agent" icon="robot">
    Members can query your community's accumulated knowledge through a dedicated AI agent.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin control" icon="shield">
    You control what gets published. Thread posts are admin-only in v1.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Member contributions" icon="people-group">
    Members contribute Brain Updates from their personal Gobi Desktop.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Creators

You create content — essays, newsletters, videos, courses. Your ideas come from everywhere: conversations, research, random observations. But the gap between having ideas and publishing them is wide, and most ideas get lost before they become anything.

Gobi Personal Space gives your Brain a public face. Share what you know, build in public, and let your knowledge compound into an audience.

**What Gobi does for you:**
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Public Brain profile" icon="user">
    Your Brain ID is your public identity — how others discover your knowledge.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Brain Posts" icon="pen-to-square">
    Publish curated insights from your Brain to your Personal Space.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Public Brain Graph" icon="diagram-project">
    Let followers explore the connections in your knowledge graph.
  </Card>
  <Card title="From private to public" icon="lock-open">
    Your Vault stays private. You choose what surfaces to the world.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
